# 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview
Supply chains generate a massive amount of data every day — but without the right tools, most of it goes unused.

In this project, I built an **interactive Tableau dashboard** to explore how revenue, profit, and delivery performance behave across a supply chain system. The idea was simple:  
👉 turn raw operational data into **clear, actionable insights**.

The dashboard focuses on:
- 📈 Revenue performance  
- 💰 Profitability trends  
- 🚚 Delivery efficiency  
- ⚙️ Key operational drivers  

This project simulates a **real-world Business Intelligence setup**, where decision-makers can quickly understand what’s working — and what’s not.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The dashboard is designed to answer practical business questions like:
- How are **revenue and profit trending year-over-year (CY vs PY)?**
- Are we meeting **on-time delivery expectations?**
- Which **regions or customers are underperforming?**
- What factors are **driving profit fluctuations?**

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included

### 1️⃣ Revenue & Profit Overview
This is the **high-level view** — built for quick decision-making.

**What you’ll find:**
- 💰 Total Revenue: **$87.7M**
- 📊 Year-over-Year growth comparison  
- 📅 Monthly profit trends with min/max highlights  
- 🗺️ State-wise revenue vs profit breakdown  
- 📉 Weekly performance vs average benchmarks  

👉 Think of this as the *executive summary* of the entire supply chain.

---

### 2️⃣ Performance Drivers Analysis
This dashboard goes one level deeper — into the **“why” behind the numbers**.

**Key insights explored:**
- 👥 How customer volume impacts profit  
- 🔄 Difference between **new vs returning customers**  
- 📦 Profit variation across freight types (quarterly view)  
- 🚛 Driver performance:
  - On-time delivery vs idle time  
- 📍 Customer-level profitability across locations  

👉 This is where patterns turn into decisions.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 🎛️ Interactive Filters
To make the dashboard truly usable, I implemented multiple filters that allow users to explore data dynamically:

- 📅 **Year Filter** – Switch between different time periods  
- 🗺️ **Location/State Filter** – Focus on specific regions  
- 👤 **Customer-level filtering** for deeper analysis  
- 📦 Context-based filtering across visuals  

👉 These filters help users move from a **high-level overview to granular insights** seamlessly.

---

### 🔗 Dashboard Navigation (Custom Icons)
Instead of relying on default navigation, I designed **custom icons/buttons** to switch between dashboards.

- 🧭 Smooth navigation between dashboards  
- 🎯 Better user experience (feels like an app, not static charts)  
- 🎨 Consistent theme using custom-designed icons  

👉 This adds a layer of **product-like experience** to the dashboard.

---

### ✅ Advanced Calculations (30+ fields)
A big part of this project was building logic behind the visuals:

- 📊 Year-over-Year Growth (YoY %)  
- 🔁 CY vs PY comparisons  
- 🚦 KPI indicators (Above / Below average)  
- ⏱️ Delivery performance metrics:
  - On-Time %
  - Delay Rate  
- 💹 Profit vs Loss classification  
- 📌 Dynamic Min/Max calculations  
- 👤 Customer segmentation (New vs Returning)  

---

## 🎨 Design Approach
I kept the design clean and business-focused:

- 🎯 Blue & White corporate theme  
- 📊 Consistent KPI cards and icons  
- 📈 Balanced mix of visuals:
  - Line charts for trends  
  - Bar charts for comparisons  
  - Donut charts for distribution  
  - Tables for detailed insights  

👉 The goal was clarity over complexity.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
A few interesting takeaways from the analysis:

- ⚠️ Some regions generate **high revenue but low profit**  
- 🚚 Delivery delays have a **direct impact on profitability**  
- 🔁 Returning customers contribute a significant share of revenue  
- 🚛 Driver efficiency plays a role in overall performance  
- 📦 Profit varies across freight types and quarters
